# 💸 Spendly

**Personal Finance Management App**

Spendly is a mobile-first personal finance management application designed to give users complete visibility and control over their daily spending. It enables fast and frictionless expense tracking combined with powerful analytics, smart categorization, and budget monitoring — all within a clean, modern interface.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📌 Expense Management

* Add, edit, and delete expenses in seconds
* Smart category-based organization
* Recurring expense support (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
* Notes and sub-category support

---

### 📊 Dashboard & Insights

* Monthly spending overview with progress tracking
* Daily and monthly expense breakdown
* Recent transactions view
* Spending comparison with previous months

---

### 📅 Calendar View

* Interactive monthly calendar with spending heatmap
* Visual indicators for high-spending days
* Tap any date to view detailed daily expenses

---

### 📈 Analytics

* Category-wise expense distribution
* Monthly trend analysis
* Top spending days identification
* Insightful summaries (auto-generated insights)

---

### 💰 Budget Management

* Set total monthly budget
* Set category-wise budgets
* Real-time budget tracking
* Alerts at 80% and 100% usage

---

### 📤 Export & Reports

* Export data in CSV format
* Generate detailed PDF reports
* Share reports easily

---

### 🔐 Authentication & Security

* Secure email/password authentication
* Biometric authentication (Face ID / Fingerprint)
* JWT-based session handling

---

### 🌐 Offline-First Experience

* Works without internet
* Local data storage using SQLite
* Automatic sync when back online

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

Spendly solves a common problem:

> People struggle to understand where their money goes.

It provides a **log → analyze → improve loop**:

1. Log expenses quickly
2. Analyze spending patterns
3. Improve financial habits through insights and budgeting

---

## 🎯 Target Users

* 🧑‍🎓 Students managing limited budgets
* 💼 Young professionals tracking expenses
* 👨‍👩‍👧 Families managing household spending
* 🧳 Freelancers needing expense records

---

## 📌 App Flow

1. User signs up / logs in
2. Completes onboarding (currency, budget, categories)
3. Adds expenses daily
4. Tracks spending via dashboard & analytics
5. Sets budgets and receives alerts
6. Exports reports when needed
